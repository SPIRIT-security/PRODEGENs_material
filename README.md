Research Data Repository
Overview

This website provides anonymized materials related to our research.
All data have been carefully anonymized to protect participant privacy and to ensure ethical transparency.
The designer-centric study (e.g., design tasks, interviews, and surveys) was reviewed and approved by the Institutional Review Board (IRB) of our institution.

Contents

The repository includes the following resources:

1. Anonymized Prompts

The input prompts used in our experiments.
These have been de-identified to remove any potentially sensitive information.

2. DP-Related Sentences

Sentences that influenced or contributed to the generation of DPs (Derived Phrases / Data Points) during the study.

3. Study Procedure

The full set of interview questions used during the data collection process.
These are provided to support reproducibility and transparency in the research methodology.

Ethical Considerations

All materials have been anonymized according to institutional and ethical guidelines.
We remain committed to maintaining the highest ethical standards in our research and welcome any feedback or suggestions that could help further improve the integrity of this work.

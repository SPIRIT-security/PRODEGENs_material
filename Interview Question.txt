  2.	What is the size of your company or team? What is your role?

  4.	Which non AI UI design tools do you usually work with (e.g., Figma)?

    a.	How often do you use it? 
    b.	Which ones do you use most often? 
    c.	Why do you use them?
    d.	Roughly what percentage of your design process involves AI-generated interfaces? 
    e.	In what ways do you typically use AI to support your work?
    f.	What do you like about AI UI tools?
    g.	What do you dislike about AI UI tools?
    h.	What problems do you encounter when using AI UI tools?


Dark Patterns 
  1.	Have you heard of the term dark patterns?
  2.	Did you notice any dark patterns in the UI design?
    a.	If yes, what were they?
    b.	If no or unsure, [explain what the DP is and which part of the UI is DP]
  3.	Why do you think the dark pattern(s) appeared in the design?
  4.	Which part of prompts would you think will result dark pattern?
  5.	What are your thoughts on the presence of such dark patterns?
  6.	How do you think users would react to these dark patterns? Would it affect their experience? Would it bring any harm to the users?
  7.	How do you think your company or your team would view these dark patterns? What actions might they take?
  8.	Should these dark patterns be kept or removed? Why?
    a.	[Regardless of whether participants agree or disagree] how to improve the prompt to avoid these dark patterns?
    b.	Are there other ways (technical or non technical, within or outside Figma) to avoid these dark patterns?
